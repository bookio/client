What is Bookio?

Bookio handles reservations, lending and booking. For you who needs to keep track on things, accommodation, people or activities. You can make the reservations yourself, or let your customers do it online.

You lend something

You run a Bed & Breakfast, has 12 Segways for rent, owns a Limousine-service or started a doggie daycare.

Start by describing what you rent with a description, pictures, price and rules for booking. As soon as this is done you are online, offering your customers instant booking 24/7.

With Bookios unique approach you can instantly and visually see what objects are free, temporarily unavailable and reserved.

You reserve someone

You run a nail salon, manages a group of personal trainers or have a speakers bureau.

Start by describing your resources, what services they provide, price and duration. All resources are now directly bookable via the web or mobile. Your customers can browse through the services available and book wherever they are.

The reservations ends up in your own calendar, and you can 'lock' times you do not want to be booked on. If you want, you can be alerted by email or text message when you get booked.

You arrange activities

You may organize tastings, holds a sailing school or are responsible for arranging a seminar at your job.

Both one-time occasions and a wide range of activities are easily managed in Bookio.

You create the activities you want. Then add photos, description, address, number of seats, date and price. The activities can be seen on your website, and booked from there.

Let the activity have a queue, this takes care of itself and if someone cancels, the reservation is automatically offered to the one 'next in line'.

You clearly see status on activities and you can book a participant yourself (there are still people who call and send e-mail). Worried about attendance? Push for the event on Twitter or Facebook and let the participants book there.

Your business online in less than 10 minutes

A few easy steps and your business is instantly online, allowing your customers to make reservations 24/7 from any location.

With Mobile Devices; Reservations can be made with smart phones, tablets or computers connected to the Internet or with SMS and Twitter.

On Your Web Page; You can easily add our Bookio component to your home page or Facebook fan page to offer online reservations.

The Old School Way; Bookio gives a clear overview of reservations and you can quickly make a reservation in response to a call, e-mail or walk-in.
